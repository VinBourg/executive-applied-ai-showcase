# Market Alignment 2026

This repository is intentionally aligned with demand visible on `April 19, 2026` across three markets:
- France
- Switzerland
- USA East Coast

The objective is not to imitate job ads mechanically.
The objective is to show a portfolio that maps to recurring market expectations with clean, readable and business-relevant examples.

## What current demand looks like

### France

Recent France listings are combining `GenAI / RAG` with industrial delivery expectations:
- Python, ML and LLM / RAG implementation,
- AWS or cloud deployment,
- evaluation, robustness and monitoring,
- data platform environments using Azure, Databricks, dbt, Snowflake and Power BI,
- KPI reporting, dashboard delivery and business translation.

## Why item 16 was added

The main gap across the three markets was not more RAG or more prompt engineering.
The main gap was `cloud data platform governance`.

That is why the repository now includes:

- `16_Cloud_Data_Platform_Governance`

This item closes a real market gap by demonstrating:
- data quality scoring,
- ownership and contracts,
- release-readiness checks,
- trusted downstream delivery for BI, analytics and AI,
- a more platform-oriented profile that reads better in France, Switzerland and the USA East Coast.

## Practical conclusion

With the addition of `16_Cloud_Data_Platform_Governance`, the repository now reads less like a compact AI showcase only, and more like a credible portfolio for:
- Applied AI consultant,
- GenAI / LLM delivery consultant,
- analytics and decision-support consultant,
- AI automation specialist,
- cloud data platform / data governance consultant,
- senior freelance profiles serving regulated or enterprise clients.
