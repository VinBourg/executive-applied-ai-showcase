# Agentic Research Briefing

A compact agentic demo that turns a target account and an objective into a structured briefing package.

## Why it matters

This example demonstrates:
- agentic research planning
- multi-step synthesis logic
- structured account briefing generation
- executive-ready preparation outputs

## Business relevance

- pre-meeting research
- account preparation
- decision-support briefing
- AI-assisted executive synthesis

## Generated outputs

- `research_brief.md`
- `opportunity_map.csv`

## Operational value

- prepares an account review faster,
- gives an advisory or sales team a ranked opportunity map,
- supports better meeting preparation with clearer angles and next steps.
