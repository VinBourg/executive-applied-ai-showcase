# Dashboard Specification - Northbank Operations

## Objective
Reduce support backlog while preserving SLA compliance and customer satisfaction.

## Primary Users
Head of Operations, Support Manager, Team Leads

## Pain Points
- Backlog is growing without a clear driver analysis
- SLA breaches are reviewed too late
- Reopened tickets consume team capacity

## Key Business Questions
- Which ticket categories drive the backlog?
- Where are SLA breaches concentrated?
- Which teams need stabilization support?

## Recommended KPIs
- Backlog volume
- SLA compliance rate
- Reopened ticket rate
- Average resolution time
- Tickets by category and team

## Recommended Filters
- date, priority, category, team

## Suggested Pages
- Executive overview
- Backlog and SLA analysis
- Team performance view
- Category and root-cause analysis

## Delivery Note
This dashboard should be designed for fast management review and clear operational follow-up rather than decorative reporting.
