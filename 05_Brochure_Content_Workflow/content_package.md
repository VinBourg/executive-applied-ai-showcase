# Content Package - Brochure Workflow

## Editorial Intent
- Keep the brochure concrete and commercially readable.
- Position the product as an execution tool, not a generic AI promise.

## Message Map
- Hero line: InsightPilot AI helps operations leaders in banking and industry reduce repetitive reporting and knowledge-search effort.
- Problem statement: Teams lose time searching for information and preparing repetitive reporting outputs.
- Solution statement: An ai assistant that combines knowledge retrieval, summarization and workflow automation.
- Call to action: Start with a focused pilot on one reporting or knowledge workflow, then scale from proven usage.

## Proof Points
- faster access to validated knowledge
- more consistent reporting outputs
- reduced manual effort on repetitive tasks

## Quality Checklist
- Mentions the target audience clearly.
- Avoids generic claims without proof points.
- Ends with a concrete next step.
